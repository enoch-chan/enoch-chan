The Sheffield Christmas market is always a yearly highlight for the city. You’ll find it located on Fargate and in the Peace Gardens in Sheffield city centre, and it's perfect for those looking to celebrate the festive season and soak up the atmosphere. Specialist wooden cabins stocked with a host of goodies readily line the streets - with fantastic choice available on sweets, treats and craft products. Decorative gifts and handmade trinkets are always in plentiful supply too, so you’ll have lots of inspiration when shopping for festive treats. The food stalls are also sure to be worth a visit, with plenty of festive grub to warm you up!
